A hub for exchanging a wide variety of goods and services, Craigslist offers a simple and user-friendly interface where individuals and businesses can post and browse listings across numerous categories such as housing, jobs, items for sale, services, and community events. With an emphasis on community engagement, Craigslist has become a popular destination for connecting people while facilitating meaningful transactions. Whether you're looking for a gig, refurbished items, or simply want to declutter your home, Craigslist provides a convenient space to connect with others and fulfill your needs.

A Brief History Lesson

1995

Feeling isolated as a newcomer to San Francisco, Craig Newmark created an email distribution list to friends for local events.

1999

Craislist incorporates, expanding beyond San Francisco and launches across the United States.

2006

Craigslist begins international expansion and becomes supported in a variety of languages.

2010

Craigslist reaches over 50 countries forming a truly global network.

Today

Craigslist continues to operate as a popular online marketplace for classified ads, serving millions of users worldwide.
